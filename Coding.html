Coding

1. Highlight your coding skills:

Clearly state your coding skills and expertise, so potential clients know what languages and technologies you are proficient in.

2. Showcase examples of previous work:

Include examples of coding projects you have worked on in the past. This could be through screenshots, live demos, or links to GitHub repositories.

3. Communicate your process:

Explain your coding process and how you work with clients to deliver their project. This can help build trust and confidence in your services.

4. Offer different service packages:

Provide different levels of coding services to clients, such as basic website development or complex web application development.

5. Provide technical documentation:

Offer documentation on the coding projects you have worked on, so clients can easily understand how the project works and what they can expect.

6. Include a contact form:

Make it easy for potential clients to get in touch with you for more information or to request your services.
